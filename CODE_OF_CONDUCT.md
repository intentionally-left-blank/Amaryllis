# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to an inclusive, respectful, and constructive community.

Expected behavior:

- Use welcoming and professional language.
- Give actionable technical feedback.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions first.
- Focus criticism on ideas and implementation, not people.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, abuse, or personal attacks.
- Discrimination or exclusionary language.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Reporting

If you need to report a conduct issue, contact maintainers through a private security/governance channel documented in `MAINTAINERS.md`.
