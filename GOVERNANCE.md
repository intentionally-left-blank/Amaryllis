# Governance

## Maintainer Model

Amaryllis is maintainer-led.

- Maintainer responsibilities are listed in `MAINTAINERS.md`.
- Maintainers review changes in their owned areas.
- Cross-cutting or high-risk changes require at least two approvals.

## Decision Process

- Small changes: decision by responsible maintainer.
- Architecture or policy changes: RFC-style issue + maintainer review.
- Security-sensitive updates: security-first review before merge.

## Release Discipline

- Release gates must be green before publish.
- Policy and compliance regressions block release.
- Rollback instructions and incident notes are required for risky changes.
